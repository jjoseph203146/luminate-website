    About

    Founder-led, Augusta-rooted.

    Luminate Web Design & Development was started to give Augusta's small businesses, ministries, and nonprofits the kind of website usually reserved for big budgets.

Today, Luminate designs and builds custom sites for businesses, churches, and nonprofits across the CSRA, and stays on as a long-term partner through its care plans.
